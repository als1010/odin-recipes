Skirt Steak

A quick and easy steak sure to rival your favorite restaurant!

Ingredients

  • Skirt steak
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Season steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Pre-heat a cast iron skillet with thyme and garlic until smoking.
  3. Place the steak in the skillet and sear for 2 minutes a side.
  4. Melt butter and baste the steak well until medium rare.
  5. Remove and let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
  6. Serve over your favorite side and enjoy!
