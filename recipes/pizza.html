Pizza

Totally radical!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Knead dough until 8 inches in diameter.
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  3. Add a uniform layer of mozzarella cheese.
  4. Bake at 400 degrees fahrenheit until the crust is golden brown.
  5. Slice as desired and enjoy!
