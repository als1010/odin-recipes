Chicken Parmesan

An Italian style dish that is a fan favorite around the world!

Ingredients

  • Sliced chicken breast
  • Seasoned bread crumbs
  • Eggs or flour
  • Canola oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breast, dip in scrambled egg and coat in seasoned bread crumbs or flour
  2. Heat canola oil and fry the chicken breast until golden brown.
  3. Remove from oil and let dry on paper towel.
  4. Place chicken breast on a baking sheet and cover with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit until the cheese is melted and let cool.
  6. Serve over your favorite pasta and enjoy!
