Chicken Parmesan

Description

A golden brown fried chicken cutlet breaded with fresh Italian breadcrumbs and seasoned with colorful sazon and black pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of thinly sliced chicken breast
  • 8 ounces of seasoned Italian breadcrumbs
  • 8 ounches of thickly sliced fresh mozzerella cheese
  • 4 ounces of finely shredded fresh parmesan cheese
  • 1 jar of Rao's Famous tomato sauce
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 fresh garlic cloves
  • Salt & pepper
  • Sazon seasoing
  • Fresh basil

Steps:

  1. Crack the two large eggs in a medium sized bowl and scramble until evenly mixed.
  2. Fill another medium sized bowl with breadcrumbs.
  3. Peel and dice the fresh garlic and add half into the eggs.
  4. Heat a medium frying pan with oil and let sit until hot.
  5. In the mean time, season your chicken cutlet breast with salt, pepper, sazon and fresh garlic.
  6. Dip a single chicken breast in the egg until gernously coated.
  7. Repeat with the breadcrumbs. Be sure to coat the entire chicken breast.
  8. Repeat for each slice of chicken breast.
  9. Gently place 2 to 3 slices of the coated chicken breast in the oil. Make sure the oil is hot enough.
  10. Cook the chicken for 3-4 minutes a side or until consistently golden brown. Do not overcook the chicken as we will finish it in the oven.
  11. Repeat for all chicken slices.
  12. Remove chicken slices on place them on the plate with paper towel to soak excess oil for 5 to 10 minutes.
  13. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Prepare cooked chicken slices on a large baking sheet.
  14. Cover chicken in a generous amount of Rao's Famous tomato sauce.
  15. Top each slice with fresh mozzerella cheese and place gently cover with more sauce.
  16. Bake until cheese is melted and remove to cool.
  17. Top with fresh basil and parmesan cheese and heat additional sauce if neccessary.
  18. Serve with pasta and garlic bread.
