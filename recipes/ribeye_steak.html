Ribeye Steak

Description

A juicy thick-cut ribeye steak seared with fresh thyme and basted with butter.

Ingredients:

  • 10 ounce ribeye steak
  • 4 ounces of fresh thyme
  • 2 table spoons of unsalted butter
  • 3 fresh garlic cloves
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Peel and dice the fresh garlic.
  2. Generously season the steak with salt, pepper and half of the garlic.
  3. Heat and spread a drizzle of olive oil in a seasoned cast iron skillet on medium heat.
  4. Add fresh thyme and garlic.
  5. When steaming, place the steak in the pan and sear for three to four minutes.
  6. Flip the seak and continue to sear. Add two tablespoons of butter and melt.
  7. Baste the steak with butter and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Remove steak after approaching desired temperature and let rest.
  9. Allow five minutes for the steak to rest before slicing.
  10. Serve with mashed potatoes and asparagus.
