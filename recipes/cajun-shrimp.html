Cajun Shrimp

Description

A juicy thick-cut ribeye steak seared with fresh thyme and basted with butter.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound raw peeled shrimp
  • Mixed fresh peppers
  • 2 table spoons of unsalted butter
  • 3 fresh garlic cloves
  • Salt & pepper
  • Cajun seasoning
  • One lemon

Steps:

  1. Peel and dice the fresh garlic.
  2. Wash and slice the mixed peppers.
  3. Generously season the shrimp with salt, pepper and cajun seasoning.
  4. Heat and spread a drizzle of olive oil in a seasoned cast iron skillet on medium heat.
  5. When steaming, place the shrimp and garlic in the pan and cook for two to three minutes.
  6. To sear, avoid stirring shrimp and instead flip with a spatula. Otherwise stir occasionally.
  7. Add mixed pepeprs and season with salt pepper and squeezed lemon.
  8. Remove shrimp after cooked through and let rest.
  9. Serve with rasta pasta and corn on the cob.
